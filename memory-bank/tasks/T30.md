## Context
The existing simplicial growth (T28) applies Pachner moves to interior simplices, placing new vertices at centroids. The paper arXiv:1108.1974v2 describes growth by gluing simplices onto boundary faces of a Cauchy hypersurface. This induces Pachner moves on the boundary while growing the complex outward. Key operations: single-simplex gluing and tent moves.
